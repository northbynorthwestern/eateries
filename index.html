A *more* comprehensive guide to Evanston eateries

Cheesie's Pub and Grub


A Northwestern classic, sure to fill your stomach (and possibly your arteries) when you're looking for a hearty grilled cheese sandwich. Be sure to come on Half-Price Wednesdays!

Price: $

Phone Number: (847) 868-8686

527 Cafe


Although it often gets overshadowed by Joy Yee's, 527 Cafe offers equally delicious Asian cusine with a much quieter and less busy venue, albeit with a slower service time.

Recommendations: Fried rice, beef noodle soup, popcorn chicken

Hours: Tue-Fri: 11:30am - 9:30 pm, Sat-Sun: 11am - 9:30 pm

Price: $

Phone Number: (847) 332-2233

Dave's Italian Kitchen


Dave's Italian Kitchen is an Italian restaurant with a warm and homely atmosphere. The food is decent, the portions are large, and you'll usually leave satisfied.

Recommendations: Chicken parmesan, ravioli, ziti

Hours: Sun-Thurs: 4pm - 10pm, Fri-Sat: 4pm - 11pm

Price: $$

Phone Number: (847) 864-6000

Lao Sze Chuan


Lao Sze Chuan is sure to satisfy your craving for authentic Chinese cuisine! If you love spicy food, be sure to order the 'Szechuan' dishes.

Recommendations: Mapo tofu, Szechuan dishes

Hours: Sun-Thurs: 11am - 10pm, Fri-Sat: 11am - 11pm

Price: $$

Phone Number: (847) 868-8989

Five Guys


Five Guys is always a solid place to go to if you're looking for a fast, cheap place to fulfill your burger craving. They also offer some of the best fries in Evanston!

Recommendations: Double Bacon Cheeseburger, fries

Price: $

Phone Number: (847) 491-6921

800 Degrees Pizza
